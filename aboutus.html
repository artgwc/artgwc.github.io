About Us

A.R.T.

A.R.T. is a community for all artists whether they be illustrators, musicians, film-makers, sculptors, mediums in order to create a community for creative people. Through this, we hope to establish a place where musicians can inspire illustrators, illustrators can inspire film-makers, and etc. We also hope to allow people to find new independent artists and just explore talents that may not be given the spotlight they deserve. We believe that through having this collaborative community, art prospers, or as our name states, Artists Rise Together.
